On our dashboard, we would like to see the following:
State of worker role
Machine counters (CPU Utilization %, RAM available)
#URL's crawled
Last 10 URL's crawled
Size of queue (#urls left in pipeline to be crawled)
Size of index (Table storage with our crawled data)
Any errors and their URL's

On the dashboard, we also want the follwing functionality:
Clear everything (index/table, queue/pipeline, stop all worker roles)
Start crawling (give a root URL, ex: www.cnn.com and kick off worker roles)
Retrieve data from index (get page title for specific URL)
